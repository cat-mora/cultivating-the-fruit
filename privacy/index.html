Privacy Policy

Who We Are

Cultivating the Fruit is a digital product available at cultivatingthefruit.com. You can reach us at info@cultivatingthefruit.com.

What Information We Collect

When you purchase through our site, we collect your name, email address, and payment details. Payment information is processed securely by Stripe and is never stored on our servers. We do not have access to your full card details.

When you visit our site, standard server and analytics data may be collected, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages. This data is used only to understand how the site is being used and to improve your experience.

If you join our email list, we hold your email address and any preferences you have indicated. We use Loops to manage email communications.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to process your purchase, deliver your product, send you relevant communications about your purchase or the app, and occasionally send you encouragement or updates related to your subscription. We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising on behalf of any other business.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our business:

Stripe — payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

Each of these services has its own privacy policy and data practices. We encourage you to review them if you have questions about how your data is handled at the infrastructure level.

Cookies

Our site may use cookies to support functionality and to understand how the site is being used. You can adjust cookie settings in your browser at any time. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, to ask us to correct inaccurate information, or to request that we delete your data. To make any of these requests, email us at info@cultivatingthefruit.com and we will respond within 14 days.

Contact
