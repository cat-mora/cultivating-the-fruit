Get the App

How do I add it to my home screen?

iPhone (Safari): Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen, then tap "Add to Home Screen."

Android (Chrome): Tap the three-dot menu in the top right corner, then tap "Add to Home Screen" then "Install App."

I don't use Safari (iPhone) or Chrome (Android)

No problem. Copy the app link below and paste it into whichever browser you prefer. Once it loads, follow the same steps above — most modern browsers on both iPhone and Android support "Add to Home Screen." Look for the Share button or your browser's menu.

https://app.cultivatingthefruit.com

I can't find "Add to Home Screen" in my browser menu

Some browsers handle this differently. Try looking for "Install App", "Add Shortcut", or a small download/install icon in your browser's address bar. If you still cannot find it, open the link in Safari (iPhone) or Chrome (Android) where the option is most reliable.

I'm on a computer, not a phone

The app is designed for phone use, but you can still access it from any browser on your computer. Go to the link below and sign up with your invite code. You won't be able to add it to a home screen, but you can bookmark it and use it from your browser.

https://app.cultivatingthefruit.com
