Getting Started

1

Install the app on your phone

Cultivating the Fruit is a web app — it installs directly from your browser, no App Store or Google Play required. Full instructions are in your welcome email.

2

Choose your stream

When you open the app for the first time, choose the Strengthen Your Marriage stream. You can access the Repair stream at any time from within the app.

3

Read today's Scripture anchor

Each day opens with a verse and a quality from the Fruit of the Spirit. Read it slowly. Let it settle before you choose your action.

4

Choose one action — and do it

You will see five options. Choose one that fits today. Something you say, something you do, a conversation, time together, or a moment between you and God first. One is enough.

A Word Before You Begin

You do not need to do anything big today. You do not need your husband to be on board. You do not need to feel inspired or certain.

You just need to open the app, read the verse, and choose one thing to do today.

That is the whole practice. Small, faithful, consistent. That is what this is built for.
