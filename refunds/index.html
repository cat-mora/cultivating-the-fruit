Our Approach to Refunds

We want you to feel completely confident purchasing Cultivating the Fruit. If the app is not practical, useful, or worth the small daily investment for you, we would rather refund you than have you hold onto something that is not helping.

We do ask that you give it a genuine month first. The benefit of a daily-action tool builds with consistent use, and most people find that things shift meaningfully within the first few weeks. Use it through the month, and if it is still not working for you, email us before the 30 days are up and we will refund you in full.

How to Request a Refund

Email us at info@cultivatingthefruit.com within 30 days of your purchase date. Include the email address you used to purchase and we will process your refund promptly. You will receive confirmation once it is done.

Refunds are processed back to your original payment method via Stripe. Depending on your bank, this typically appears within 5 to 10 business days.
